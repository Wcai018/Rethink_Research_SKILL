## Cross-domain Search Tips

When checking co-occurrence across two fields A and B:
1. Search `"[A keyword] [B keyword]"` in Semantic Scholar or Google Scholar
2. Filter to last 2 years and top venues only
3. Count papers where both appear in title or abstract
4. If <5% of papers in either field mention both → co-occurrence is weak

For citation graph checks:
- Use Semantic Scholar's "References" and "Citations" panels
- Check if top-10 cited papers in field A cite any top-10 in field B
- No overlap = citation gap confirmed
